If YOU could find paradise, would it be everything you hoped for?

“People of all nations, I invite you to a paradise unlike any place you have seen, where fresh fruit and green pastures roll over the hills, and the air smells of honey and wine. Follow the trail of the Emerald Earl, and in their footsteps find the Golden Grove.” With this proclamation, the Ruby Regal ascended towards the Golden Grove, never to be seen again. A new age of adventuring has begun, as travelers from around the swampy and polluted realm of Lazulus search for the last resting place of the Great Gem Guardians, and the secret dangers they hid within..

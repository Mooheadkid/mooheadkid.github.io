Exalted vs. Tarnished

There exists a very simple difference between the Exalted of Vivre, and the Tarnished.
The Exalted and their ancestors have retained the favor of the Elders.
As for being Tarnished, there are two ways of becoming so. One is to inherit Tarnished status; the other is as a punishment from the Elders.
In terms of differences between Exalted or Tarnished, it is said that the Elders can keenly sense a person's Exalted Status, or lack there of.

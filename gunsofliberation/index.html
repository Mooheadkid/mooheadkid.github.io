The Revolution Returns...

The guns of liberation rang loud on that day, the Battle of Eagle’s Claw, but their cries were cut short by a decisive defeat from the Colonial Government. Rounded up and executed, the leaders of the rebellion vanished from the history books, never to be spoken of again. Yet their specters of freedom live on, and as the Colonial Government grows weak, new ideas will challenge the old, as one last clash of titans will change the face of Cóndor forever. Let the spark of rebellion, and the cry of freedom ring out from the mouths of the weary! ¡Viva la revolución! ¡Viva la república! ¡Viva Cóndor!
